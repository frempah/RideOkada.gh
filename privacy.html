OkadaRide is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Who We Are

OkadaRide Ghana is a tricycle ride-booking app operating in Sunyani, Brong-Ahafo Region, Ghana.

2. Information We Collect

When you use OkadaRide as a passenger:

  • Full name — to identify you to your rider
  • Phone number — for account login and WhatsApp notifications
  • Profile photo — so riders can identify you at pickup
  • Pickup and dropoff locations — to process your ride booking
  • Payment preference — Cash or Mobile Money
  • Trip history — record of your past rides

For riders, we additionally collect: tricycle plate number, colour, year, Ghana Card number, and tricycle photo for identity verification.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm ride bookings
  • To send WhatsApp notifications about your ride
  • To verify rider identity and ensure passenger safety
  • To maintain your trip history
  • To improve the OkadaRide service

4. How We Protect Your Data

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted on encrypted European servers. All connections use HTTPS. PINs are hashed with SHA-256 — we never store plain-text passwords.

  • HTTPS encryption on all connections
  • PINs stored as SHA-256 hashes — never plain text
  • Database protected by Firestore security rules
  • We do not sell your data to any third party

5. Data Sharing

We share your data only as needed to provide the service:

  • With your rider — name, phone and photo shared so they can identify you
  • OkadaRide dispatcher — booking details for operations via WhatsApp
  • Google Firebase — our secure database provider

We do not sell, rent or share your data with advertisers or other third parties.

6. WhatsApp Communications

OkadaRide sends booking confirmations and ride updates via WhatsApp to your registered number. By registering you consent to receive these messages.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account

Contact frempahvanasco03@gmail.com to exercise any of these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

OkadaRide is for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept any updates.
